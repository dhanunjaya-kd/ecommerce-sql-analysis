# Business Insights

## Revenue Performance

* The platform generated **16.01M** in total revenue across **99K orders**.
* Revenue concentration analysis showed that a small number of product categories contributed a significant share of overall sales.
* The average order value was **160.99**, indicating healthy transaction sizes across the platform.

## Customer Analysis

* The business served approximately **96K unique customers**.
* Customer distribution was heavily concentrated in major urban regions, with a few states contributing the majority of customers and orders.
* Customer acquisition opportunities remain in lower-penetration regions.

## Product Performance

* Top-performing product categories consistently generated a large share of platform revenue.
* Product category analysis revealed a clear gap between high-performing and low-performing categories, suggesting opportunities for portfolio optimization.

## Order Trends

* Order volume remained relatively stable through most of the reporting period.
* Revenue trends closely followed order trends, indicating that sales performance was primarily driven by transaction volume.

## Payment Insights

* Credit Card was the dominant payment method and represented the majority of transactions.
* Voucher and Debit Card transactions accounted for only a small percentage of total payments.

## Logistics & Freight

* Total freight expenditure reached **2.25M**.
* Several product categories generated disproportionately high freight costs, indicating potential opportunities to improve shipping efficiency and logistics planning.

## Key Business Recommendations

1. Invest further in top-performing product categories to maximize revenue growth.
2. Expand marketing efforts in underrepresented regions to increase customer acquisition.
3. Optimize logistics operations for categories with the highest freight expenses.
4. Increase customer retention initiatives to improve repeat purchase rates.
5. Monitor category-level performance regularly to identify emerging growth opportunities.
