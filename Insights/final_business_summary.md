# Business Summary

## Revenue
- Total Revenue generated: ₹13.59 Million
- Average Order Value: ₹137.75

## Customers
- Total Customers: 99,441
- Customer concentration is highest in São Paulo (SP), followed by Rio de Janeiro (RJ) and Minas Gerais (MG).

## Products
- Beauty & Health generated the highest revenue.
- Watches & Gifts and Bed/Bath products were among the strongest performing categories.

## Payments
- Credit Card is the dominant payment method.
- Boleto is the second most used payment method.
- Voucher and Debit Card account for a much smaller share of transactions.

## Conclusion
The business generates strong revenue from a relatively concentrated customer base, with credit card payments dominating transactions and a small number of product categories contributing a significant portion of total sales.
